Embrace technology's solution to education

With Stanford, MIT, Harvard, and a growing number of visionary schools and Silicon Valley startups generously sharing a new breed of highly interactive online courses, we believe that the best Computer Science education may soon be available for free online. But nobody wants to pursue higher education, socially detached, alone in front of the computer screen.


This summer Silicon Stockholm will introduce online students to their classmates by providing a physical environment for study. Together, we will unravel the online trove of educational computer science content and apply our skills to create original software.

 

A glorious new dawn awaits for education. Be the first to embrace it.
